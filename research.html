F C Kohli Day

The Kohli Centre on Intelligent Systems (KCIS) commemorated the birth anniversary of Sri F C Kohli with a special program celebrating his life through research and innovation on 20 March. The event had the following events:

  • Two keynote talks, the first was by Prof. Subbarao Kambhampati, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, School of Computing & AI, Arizona State University and the second keynote talk was by Prof. V S Subrahmanian, Walter P Murphy Professor of Computer Science Faculty Fellow at the Northwestern Buffett Institute for Global Affairs.
  • A technical panel with panellists - Dr. Rachana Dave, MicroGO; Rachana Tripathi CEO at Huwel Lifesciences Hyderabad; Ashutosh Richhariya Scientist, Entrepreneur, Tractrix Optodynamics and Swarup Medasani Director of Engineering at Mathworks
  • An alumni interaction with Dr. Dip Sankar Banerjee, Assistant Professor, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur; Ishan Misra, Research Scientist at Facebook AI Research and Divya Sai Jitta, Shell Technology Centre.
