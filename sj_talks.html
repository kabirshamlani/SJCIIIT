The Silver Jubilee Talk series includes interactive sessions with highly accomplished thought leaders. First in the series was a fireside chat with Sri KT Rama Rao, Hon’ble Minister for IT, Industries, Municipal Administration and Urban Development, Government of Telangana. An evening conversation between the Minister and the students, faculty and key stakeholders.
The event was also attended by IIITH’s Governing Council Chair Prof Raj Reddy, GC members Mr Jayesh Ranjan, Mr Ajit Rangnekar and Mr Srini Raju; IIITH’s first Director Prof Narendra Ahuja, and Prof Limbadri (Chair of TS Council for Higher Education)
Shri K T Rama Rao also viewed research demos of some of IIITH’s key projects in the areas of Robotics, Language technology, Computer vision, Sustainability/Smart Cities & Research start-ups.
